Frequently Asked Questions

The best type of irrigation system depends on the size and type of your garden, as well as the plants you have. Drip irrigation is ideal for gardens with diverse plants, while sprinkler systems work well for lawns. It's best to consult with a professional to assess your specific needs.

It's recommended to service your irrigation system at least once a year, ideally before the start of the irrigation season. Regular maintenance helps ensure optimal performance and can prevent costly repairs.

Common signs include uneven water distribution, puddles or dry spots in your lawn, unusually high water bills, and visible leaks or damaged equipment. If you notice any of these issues, professional attention may be needed.

While it is possible to install an irrigation system yourself, it can be a complex process requiring knowledge of plumbing and landscape design. Hiring a professional ensures proper installation and optimal system performance.

To increase water efficiency, consider using a smart controller that adjusts watering based on weather conditions, fixing leaks promptly, using drip irrigation where appropriate, and ensuring your system is properly zoned and maintained.
